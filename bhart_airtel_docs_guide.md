## 6. STRATEGIC PLANS & CAPEX GUIDANCE

### Capital Expenditure Plans

**FY2025 (Actual)**:
- India Capex: Rs. 30,270 crores
- Operating Free Cash Flow: Rs. 31,400 crores
- Major Deployment: 20,000+ towers; 60,000+ base stations; 44,000 km fiber

**FY2026 Guidance** (From Q2 FY26 Earnings Call):
- Trend: Moderating/declining from FY25 levels
- Quarterly Capex (Q2 FY26): Rs. 7,200 crores
- Annual Estimate: Rs. 28,000-30,000 crores (moderating from Rs. 30K+)
- Drivers:
  - Rural 5G rollout substantially lower (footprint largely complete)
  - Radio capex down (focus on challenger circles: MP, Maharashtra, Gujarat)
  - Transport capex steady
  - Home pass/broadband capex modest increase
  - Data center capex possible bump (Nxtra scaling; Google partnership)

---

### Strategic Focus Areas (From AGM & Earnings Calls)

**Five Pillars of Strategy**:
1. **Build Diversified & Resilient Portfolio**
   - Africa (26% of revenues)
   - India Mobile (54%)
   - India Non-Mobile (13%)
   - Indus Towers (7%)

2. **Win Quality Customers**
   - Broadband: 2.5M home passes/quarter target
   - Mobile: Postpaid, smartphone upgrades, international roaming
   - B2B: Connectivity + adjacencies (Cloud, Cybersecurity, IoT)

3. **Obsession with Brilliant Customer Experience**
   - 1km x 1km network grid view (million grids)
   - Advanced to 100m x 100m grid for precise issue identification
   - AI/ML for performance improvement
   - Anti-spam: 57B spam calls identified; 69% reduction in financial losses

4. **Build & Leverage Digital Capabilities**
   - Platform approach for all products
   - AI at core of operations

5. **War on Waste**
   - FY25: Rs. 2,200+ crores network opex saved
   - AI/ML for efficiency

---

### Data Center & Cloud Expansion

**Nxtra (Data Center Division) Plans**:
- Current Capacity: ~250 MW
- Target Capacity: 1 GW (4x expansion)
- Timeline: Substantial capacity online in FY26-27
- Investment: Rs. 1,500 Cr+ per year (stepping up)
- Multiple data centers in build stage

**Google Partnership (Vishakhapatnam AI Data Hub)**:
- Purpose-built AI-focused data center
- Terrestrial fiber network: Vishakhapatnam-Delhi-Mumbai
- Cable Landing Station (CLS) infrastructure
- Co-investment model; Airtel one of the partners

**Airtel Cloud (Xtelify)**:
- Launch: June 2025
- Type: Telco-grade, sovereign cloud (control plane in India)
- Customer Conversations: 70+ large customers (Q2 FY26)
- Signed Deals: 6 (BFSI, manufacturing, distribution)
- Delivery Center: Pune
- Partnership: IBM (for IBM Power Systems migration)
